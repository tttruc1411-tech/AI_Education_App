# 📋 Requirements: KDI Interactive Tutor (Navy & Gold Edition)

## 1. Feature Overview
A premium "Lesson Mode" add-on for the KDI AI Coding Lab. This mode uses a **Custom KDI Robot** to guide students through story-based chapters with a strict validation system and heart-based grading.

---

## 2. KDI Brand Identity
*   **Color Palette**: Primary = **Navy (#0f172a)**, Accent = **Gold (#fbbf24 / #d97706)**.
*   **Character**: A custom-designed pixel-art **KDI Robot** based on the "Hatchling" egg shell logo.
*   **Typography**: Clean, professional fonts to match KDI Education standards.

---

## 3. Gameplay & Grading (The Heart System)
*   **Requirement**: Each lesson starts with **5 Hearts** (❤️).
*   **Strict Validation**: Students CANNOT move to Step 2 until Step 1 is correct.
*   **Penalty**: 
    *   An execution error or empty step = **-1 Heart**.
    *   Using a "Direct Solution" = **-3 Hearts**.
*   **Final Grade**: Calculated based on remaining hearts (e.g., 5 Hearts = ⭐⭐⭐).
*   **Goal**: Encourage precision and reduce "guess-and-check" coding.

---

## 4. Adaptive Story Canvas
*   **Requirement**: The Right Column dynamically swaps between the **Story Playground** and the **Live Webcam**.
*   **Logic**:
    *   *Early Rounds*: Full 2D Animation (Pixel Playground).
    *   *Intermediate*: Overlap (Character reacting to camera data).
    *   *Final Round (Boss)*: Full Webcam view for real-world application.
*   **Goal**: Scaffold the learning from virtual to physical reality.
