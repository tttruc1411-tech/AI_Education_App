# Lesson 2 - Step 4: Resize Image
## Task
Resize camera images.

## Instructions
1. Initialize the camera
2. In the loop, get a frame
3. Use `resize_image()` with `width=320`, `height=240` to shrink the image
4. Display the resized image
5. Close the camera

## Required Functions
- `Init_Camera()` - Initialize camera
- `Get_Camera_Frame(capture_camera)` - Get frame
- `resize_image(input_image, width, height)` - Resize image
- `Show_Image(camera_frame)` - Display image
- `Close_Camera(capture_camera)` - Close camera

## Hints
- Shrinking images helps process faster
- Enlarging images may reduce quality
- Common sizes: 320x240, 640x480, 1280x720
