# Lesson 1 - Step 5: Rotate Image
## Task
Rotate the camera image by 90 degrees.

## Instructions
1. Initialize the camera
2. In the loop, get a frame
3. Use `rotate_image()` with `angle=90` to rotate the image
4. Display the rotated image
5. Close the camera

## Required Functions
- `Init_Camera()` - Initialize the camera
- `Get_Camera_Frame(capture_camera)` - Get a frame
- `rotate_image(input_image, angle)` - Rotate the image
- `Show_Image(camera_frame)` - Display the image
- `Close_Camera(capture_camera)` - Close the camera

## Hints
- `angle=90` to rotate 90 degrees counter-clockwise
- `angle=180` to rotate 180 degrees (upside down)
- `angle=270` to rotate 270 degrees (or -90 degrees)
