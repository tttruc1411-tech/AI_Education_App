# Lesson 2 - Step 1: Image Filters Lab
## Task
Apply grayscale filter to camera images.

## Instructions
1. Initialize the camera
2. In the loop, get a frame
3. Use `convert_to_gray()` to convert image to grayscale
4. Display the converted image
5. Close the camera

## Required Functions
- `Init_Camera()` - Initialize camera
- `Get_Camera_Frame(capture_camera)` - Get frame
- `convert_to_gray(input_image)` - Convert to grayscale
- `Show_Image(camera_frame)` - Display image
- `Close_Camera(capture_camera)` - Close camera

## Hints
- Grayscale images have only 1 color channel instead of 3 (RGB)
- Grayscale images are often used for faster image processing
